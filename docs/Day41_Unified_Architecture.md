# Unified Scoring Engine – Zecpath AI

## Objective
The Grand Unifier. This module compresses the entire Zecpath candidate journey (ATS Resume Parsing, Initial Screening, and Deep AI Interviewing) into a single `0-100%` Hiring Intelligence Score.

## Architectural Upgrades (Phase 5)
1. **Dynamic Prioritization:** The system shifts its mathematical priorities based on the role. For "Freshers", it weighs the Deep Interview at 50% to test cognitive ability, recognizing their ATS Resume will naturally be weak.
2. **The "Veto/Dealbreaker" Engine:** A critical safety upgrade. Unlike standard averaging algorithms, Zecpath enforces minimum threshold requirements. If a candidate scores 100% on their interview but scores 20% on screening (e.g., lacking mandatory work permits), the Veto Engine immediately caps their score and triggers an automatic `REJECT`. This prevents unqualified candidates from slipping through via math manipulation.
