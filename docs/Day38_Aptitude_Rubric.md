# Aptitude & Cognitive Logic Design – Zecpath AI

## Objective
To elevate the AI from basic communication grading to complex cognitive assessment. This module evaluates how a candidate structures their thoughts, handles crises, and mitigates risk.

## The Cognitive Scoring Formula
**Final Aptitude Score** = `(Structured Thinking × 40%) + (Risk Awareness × 20%) + (Scenario Alignment × 40%)`

## Metric Definitions
| Metric | Description | How it is calculated |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Structured Thinking** | Step-by-step logic | Detects sequencing ("first", "next") and cause-and-effect ("because", "therefore"). |
| **Risk Awareness** | Trade-off evaluation | Advanced metric: Rewards candidates who discuss "fallbacks", "alternatives", or "risks". |
| **Scenario Alignment** | Crisis resolution | Checks the transcript against ideal problem-solving milestones (e.g., in a conflict, did they mention "listening" and "aligning"?). |

## Ideal Answer Format (STAR+)
The AI expects the highest-scoring candidates to follow this flow:
1. **Assess:** Understand the problem.
2. **Plan (Step-by-Step):** "First, I will... Next, I will..."
3. **Risk Check:** "The risk here is X, so my fallback plan is Y."
4. **Execute:** Resolve the scenario.
